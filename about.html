About us

James O'Brien Library, Readers Services department supports the academic snd research activities of the university. With this, the department aim to provide optimum support in teaching and facilitating activities where the utilization of library materials - print and non-print, electronic and online resources will be properly access or use.


This portal has five major modules - (1) General Library Orientation, (2) Access and Location, (3) Electronic and Online Resources, (4) Citation Styles and (5) TURNITN - where each modules have sub-modules not to overwhelm the participants in digesting info.


The University library is looking forward for a positive response towards this endeavor.
